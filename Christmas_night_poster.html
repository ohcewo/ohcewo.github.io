Krakow Christmas Markets 2024 Dates. The Krakow Christmas Markets 2024 will be held from 29 November 2024 to 1 January 2025 in the Main Market Square (Rynek Główny).Opening hours are from 10 am to 8 pm, but some stalls stay open until late, especially the food stalls which continue to serve until at least 10 pm. As the Christmas period approaches, Krakow transforms into a captivating winter wonderland. With the Old Town’s medieval streets providing the backdrop, wander 80 market stalls picking up unusual decorations while sampling traditional Polish dishes such as a cheese made from sheep’s milk. The tour of Krakow is approx. 2 hours long (with breaks), is done on foot and does include some cobbled streets.
